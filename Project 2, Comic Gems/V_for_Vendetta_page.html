You may know that Alan Moore wrote Watchmen, the most celebrated graphic novel. He also wrote The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen. What if I were to tell you that Moore wrote another legendary comic recognized by many comic book fans. V for Vendetta tells the story of a mysterious man in a white porcelain mask who fights political oppressors through terrorism and absurd acts. Later adapted into film, it is recommended by many that you read this book before you see the film. Reserve your copy at Amazon.com. If your lucky, maybe you'll get a copy that has a Guy Fawkes mask for you to wear!
